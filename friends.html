Meet Mario Friends

Here are some Mario's notable friends and their basic characteristics:

luigi

Mario's brother. He's taller and thinner than marios, often depicted as more cautious and timid but very brave when it counts. He wears a green hat and overalls.

Princess Peach

The ruler of the Mushroom Kingdo. Known for her kindness,grace,and leadershio. Often the damsel in distress but also takes an active role in various adventures. She wears a pink dress and crown.

Yoshi

A friendly dinosaur who helps Mario by giving him rides and using his long tongue to eat enemies and obstacles. Known for his loyalty and cheerful demeanor. Yoshi comes in various colors, but green is the most iconic

Toad

A loyal attendant to princess Peach and a resident of the Mushroom Kingdom. Known for his mushroom-shaped head and blue vest. He's helpful,brave, and always eager to assist.

Bowser

He's a large, and fearsome creature a dragon-turtle hybrid.Known for his imposing size,spike shell and fiery breath, Browser's main goal is often to kidnap Princess Peach and conquer Musgroom Kingdom.
